Coffee

We work with specialty coffee from current crops which is carefully roasted. Our coffee is fruity, juicy and never boring. We bring you the best from leading European specialty roasteries, currently mostly from Square Mile Coffee Roasters, a multi award winning coffee roasting company based in East London.

You can currently buy these beans in 350g bags.

La Piramide
(COLOMBIA)

apple pie / raisin / cocoa

A returning gem from the Cauca region with flavours of toffee and apple pie, leading into raisins and cocoa notes for a sweet, moreish cup!

Los Girasoles
(Costa Rica)

red plum / shortbread / caramel

We're excited to welcome back the Los Girasoles from the Tarrazu region of Costa Rica. All red plums up front with a lingering caramel sweetness and shortbread notes, it's silky, light and delicious!

Huye Mountain
(RWANDA)

strawberry / chocolate / milkshake

With distinct notes of strawberry, accompanied by sweet chocolate and a creamy milkshake mouthfeel we cannot get enough of this natural coffee!

Las Brisas
(COLOMBIA)

red grape / almond nougat / honey

Opening with delicious red grape notes followed by smooth nutty nougat which rounds off into honey sweetness, welcome back Las Brisas!
